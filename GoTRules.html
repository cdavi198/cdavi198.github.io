Rules

Players will start with a $100 budget.

Each draftable character will have a monetary value attached, as shown on the Characters page.

You can draft as many players as your budget allows.

Some teams may have some of the same characters depending on how people want to spend their budget

There will be no dropping of characters throughout the season, so you’ll never receive any bid money back in order to pick up other characters mid-season.

There is no trading available, once your team is picked it’s set.

Make sure to check the Scoring page before selecting your characters.

If one of your characters is killed, you won't be able to replace that character.
